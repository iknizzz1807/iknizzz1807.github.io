B2B Engagement & Legal FAQ

How we work together securely, legally, and efficiently. Bypassing HR bottlenecks while ensuring absolute compliance and IP protection.

1. Tax Compliance & Worker Misclassification

Hiring international talent often raises concerns about IRS compliance and worker misclassification penalties. I operate as a strictly independent B2B contractor (a business entity), not an employee.

  • W-8BEN / W-8BEN-E Provided: For US clients, I provide a signed W-8BEN (or W-8BEN-E for my corporate entity) upfront. This certifies my foreign status, completely exempting your company from the IRS 30% withholding tax on foreign-source services.
  • Zero HR Overhead: Because this is a B2B vendor relationship, you do not need to pay payroll taxes, health insurance, or benefits.
  • No EOR Required: You do not need to use expensive Employer of Record (EOR) platforms like Deel, Turing, or Remote to hire me.

2. Master Service Agreement & VC-Compliant IP Transfer

Investors (VCs) will heavily scrutinize your cap table and IP ownership during due diligence. It is critical that your company owns 100% of its codebase without any "floating" IP liens.

  • Immediate IP Assignment: Unlike amateur freelancers who hold code hostage until payment, my Master Service Agreement (MSA) utilizes a "Present Assignment with a Condition Subsequent". Intellectual property is assigned to you immediately upon creation, ensuring your VC chain-of-title is absolutely pristine.
  • Springing Reversion for Non-Payment: To protect my business, I retain a springing reversionary right. If commercial invoices remain unpaid past 30 days, the commercial license to use the unpaid code is automatically suspended until clearance.
  • NDAs: I am happy to sign your company's Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before we discuss any proprietary architecture.

3. Payment Milestones via Wise

To eliminate risk for both parties, all engagements are structured around clear, deliverable-based milestones. Invoicing is handled seamlessly via Wise Business.

  • 30% Upfront Deposit: Secures my time in the schedule and initiates the architecture planning.
  • Development & Staging: I build the module and deploy it to a staging server (or your test environment) for your team to review and test.
  • 70% Final Payment: Once you approve the staging deployment, the final commercial invoice is issued.
  • Code Handover: Upon receipt of the final payment via Wise, the source code is pushed directly to your GitHub repository and IP is transferred.

4. Async-First Workflow

I operate as a plug-and-play extension of your engineering team. You don't need to micromanage me.

  • Direct Integration: I join your Slack/Discord workspace as a guest contractor.
  • No Useless Meetings: We replace daily stand-ups with asynchronous updates. You get concise, written reports on what was built, what's blocking, and next steps.
  • GitHub Commits: Progress is tracked via clean, atomic GitHub commits and documented Pull Requests.

5. AI Liability Caps & Dispute Resolution

Building Artificial Intelligence and algorithmic infrastructure introduces asymmetric risks far beyond traditional software development.

  • AI-Specific Liability Carve-Outs: My standard MSA caps direct financial damages to 100% of the specific Statement of Work (SOW) value. All consequential and indirect damages are explicitly excluded.
  • Third-Party Model Safe Harbors: The MSA contains a strict disclaimer regarding the probabilistic nature of machine learning and LLM hallucinations. Furthermore, clients are required to provide absolute indemnification against copyright/GDPR claims arising directly from the proprietary data they supply to fine-tune or prompt the models.
  • Flexible Governing Law: I typically work with standard US (Delaware/California) or UK/EU jurisdictions using your company's boilerplate agreements. My goal is to use clean, standard contracts so we can bypass legal friction and start building immediately.
